# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

miniMDM is an open and welcoming project. Everyone who participates — through issues, pull requests, discussions, or any other channel — is expected to treat others with respect and professionalism.

## Expected Behaviour

- Be considerate and respectful in all communication.
- Give and accept constructive feedback graciously.
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users.
- Assume good intent; ask for clarification before assuming the worst.

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Public or private harassment of any kind.
- Sharing others' private information without their consent.
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces: GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other forum associated with the project.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the project maintainer. All reports will be reviewed and investigated. The maintainer reserves the right to remove comments, close issues, or ban participants who violate these standards.
